Open positions

We're expanding our core team in Zurich. We hire slowly and deliberately to keep the bar high and the environment intensely collaborative.

We look for a combination of strong technical ability and obsessive curiosity about AI and machine learning. Funding and space are available for a team of up to ten researchers, and we invest heavily in everyone we bring on.

Research Engineer

What we expect

  • Strong background in machine learning and deep learning.
  • Hands-on experience with large language models and transformer architectures.
  • Comfort with test-time computation, active inference, system-2 reasoning, or related ideas.

How to apply

Send your resume and a short introduction to benjamin@tufa.ai. Highlight the experiments you're proudest of and the systems you've shipped.

Location

Our offices are in Zurich (Limmatquai). We only consider candidates willing to relocate within six months. For visa reasons, it is significantly easier for us to hire people currently in Switzerland or the EU/EFTA.
