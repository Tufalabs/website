We have moved into our new office at Nüschelerstrasse 30 in Zurich.

The extra space gives us a better environment for focused research, collaboration, and day-to-day work across the team. We are especially excited about the new opportunities that come with the move, including a more flexible work environment and even our own gym in the office.

This new setup gives us room to grow and a stronger base for the next chapter of Tufa Labs.
