Open Positions

We look for a combination of strong technical ability and obsessive interest in AI/ML. We have the financing and office space for up to 14 people.
We will hire methodically and slowly to increase the chances that everyone has a strong positive impact and is a good fit.

Research Engineer

Requirements

  • Deep understanding of reinforcement learning, deep learning, the inner workings of large language models and transformer architectures.
  • Ability to build and improve large-scale systems for reinforcement learning and deep learning, spanning both training and inference.
  • Strong systems engineering skills for large model workloads, including performance, reliability, and the practical constraints of memory, bandwidth, batching, cache behavior, parallelism, and numerical precision.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with advanced AI concepts such as test-time computation, active inference, or system 2 reasoning.
  • Experience working on the ARC challenge or closely related problems.
  • Familiarity with tooling and infrastructure such as Slime, vLLM, Verl, Slurm, and NVFP4. Our cluster includes DGX B200 and NVL GB300 systems.

To apply for any of these positions, please send your resume and a brief introduction to benjamin@tufa.ai.

Our offices are located in Zurich in Limmatquai. We will only consider candidates willing to relocate to Zurich within six months. For work visa reasons, it is much more convenient for us to hire people either in Switzerland or in the EU/EFTA.
